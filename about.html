About Me

Originally from Brighton, England, I grew up in a vibrant city, overflowing with music, arts and culture. I have always been interested in all forms of art, originally producing music from around the age of 15 and attending a music college.
Being a strong supporter and advocate for animal rights, one of my true passions is nature, wildlife and travel. During my first trip to India is when I really picked up a camera and started to take my photography more seriously and this bought me back to the animals and our natural surroundings. With the world ever-changing, I believe that our surroundings and everything in it should be documented before this all changes for future generations.
